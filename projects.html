Projects

This is a curated list of projects that I have worked on throughout the years.

OpenSim

I was once a core developer in the open source OpenSimulator virtual worlds framework. My many contributions include: contributing a parser for scripting languages, performance improvements, migration support, and reporting and fixing many bugs.

Simple Arpeggiator

This arpeggiator plugin for music production is based on the LV2 standard. It is inspired by the LMMS standard arpeggiator effect and can be added to effect chains in hosts such as QTractor to provide interesting bass lines and similar effects.
